This chicken biryani recipe has been one of the most requested reader recipes and I'm so excited to share it with you today.
Succulent, juicy pieces of chicken are cooked in a yogurt marinade and then layered with crispy onions, coriander, mint and basmati rice
to give you a dish that the entire family will enjoy. I've broken the process step by step and made a recipe video for this chicken biryani
so that you can make this restaurant style biryani at home!
